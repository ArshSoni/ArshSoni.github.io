About Shyam Joshi

SJ Magic is an award winning magician and a full member of The Magic Circle. He has over 10 years professional experience in making any event unforgettable

He began his journey at the age of 11 and pursued his passion to become one of the most talented Asian magicians.

He has entertained from weddings to corporate events, even celebrites around the world.

Magic, Hypnosis & Mentalism - it is the perfect ice breaker. SJ Magic can perform close up, mix and mingle, table to table or even on stage completely customizable for your event.

SJ's charming style and mind-blowing magic will make any event unforgettable
